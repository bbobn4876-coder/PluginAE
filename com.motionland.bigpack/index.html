        FAQ
        Import\open the project

        1. “Open only” - will open project the same way, as File>Open menu

        2. “Import and Add” - will import the project and add the layers into the timeline

        3. “Import and Open” - will import the composition and open the related composition.

        Switching between the folders
        You can switch between the categories as you are working with file manager, or quickly switch between the folders by “click and drag” “Folders” button.
        Create collections and add to Favourites
        Also you can group favourite elements into collections or mark them as favourite.
        Applying Presets
        To apply preset, just open the composition, select your layer and drag the preset into one of the options “In”, “Out” or “In+Out”
        Adding own projects and presets
        You can do that in full version of AEViewer. More details on http://aeviewer.com
        Font Manager script doesn't open
        Relaunch AEViewer and try again.
